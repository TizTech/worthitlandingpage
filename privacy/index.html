Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy explains how Worth It? (the app and website) is operated by tizzle. For company information and general updates, refer to tizzle.org.

Information We Process

  • App data you enter (such as pay setup and price inputs) is stored locally on your device for app functionality.
  • If you email us, we process your email address and message content so we can respond.
  • Our website host may collect basic technical logs (for example IP address, browser type, and request timing) for security and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the app and website.
  • To respond to support, privacy, and legal requests.
  • To prevent abuse, investigate incidents, and improve service reliability.

Tracking and Advertising

Worth It? does not run third-party advertising trackers in the app and does not sell personal information.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers strictly to operate the app or website (for example hosting providers and Apple distribution infrastructure).

Data Retention

Data stored in the app remains on your device unless removed by you. Support emails and related records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for support, legal, and operational purposes.

Your Choices and Rights

You can stop using the app at any time and delete local app data by removing the app from your device. You can request access, correction, or deletion of support-email data by contacting info@tizzle.org.

Children's Privacy

Worth It? is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@tizzle.org. For broader company information, refer to tizzle.org.
