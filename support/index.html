Support

Need help with Worth It? Contact us and we usually reply within 2 business days.

Contact Support

Email info@tizzle.org and include:

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • A short description of the issue and steps to reproduce it.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if relevant.

Billing and Refunds

App purchases are handled by Apple. Refund requests and billing disputes are managed through your Apple ID purchase history and Apple's policies.

Privacy and Legal Requests

For privacy, legal, or policy-related requests, email info@tizzle.org with the subject line "Legal Request".

Security Reports

If you discover a security issue, email info@tizzle.org with detailed reproduction steps. Please do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before we can investigate.

Company Information

For broader company details and announcements, refer to tizzle.org.
