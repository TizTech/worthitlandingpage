Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

Worth It? is designed to work without creating an account. We do not sell personal data, and we do not run third-party ad tracking inside the app.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores your calculator preferences and income setup locally on your device so the app can remember your settings between sessions.

This local data stays on your device unless you back up your device through Apple services under your own account settings.

Information We Collect

We do not require account registration and we do not collect your personal profile information for app use.

If you contact support by email, we receive the information you choose to include (such as your email address and message content) so we can respond.

Analytics and Tracking

Worth It? does not use ad-tech trackers to follow your behavior across other apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

Worth It? is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact info@tizzle.org.
